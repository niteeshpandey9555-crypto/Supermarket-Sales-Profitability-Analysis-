# Supermarket Sales & Profitability Analysis (SQL + Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the sales performance and 
profitability of a supermarket chain across three branches. 
It includes data cleaning, SQL-based deep-dive analysis, 
and a professional Power BI Dashboard for visualization.

## Key Insights
- **Total Revenue:** $322,966.75
- **Top Category:** Food and beverages generated the highest revenue.
- **Profitability:** Health and Beauty maintains the highest gross income margins.
- **Customer Behavior:** Members contribute significantly to sales compared
to normal customers.
- **Preferred Payment:** E-wallet and Cash are almost equally preferred.

## Dashboard Pages
1. **Executive Summary:** Overall business KPIs and top performers.
2. **Sales & Profit Analysis:** Monthly trends and branch-wise performance.
3. **Customer Insights:** Detailed look into customer types, ratings, and payment habits.
